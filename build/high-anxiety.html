High Anxiety: How We Coach and Inspire

How we engage is as important to our mission as the lessons themselves. We recognize that each individual may have different learning abilities, they may assimilate at a different pace and have many different limitations.

Anxiety plays a significant role in the lives of many of these participants so we must adjust accordingly. We also believe it is important that the skills we coach are universally applicable so we follow a two tiered approach.

 

Engage

  • First we engage groups for introducing skills, demonstrating them and repeating this demonstration.
  • This is followed by individual practice.

The importance of practice is paramount. Not only does this require effort from the participant, but it also helps coaches recognize each particular participant's strengths and weaknesses.

We continue to work with the individual for as long as needed to overcome their hurdles and show progress. Once they achieve a basic understanding and proficiency they are paired with a mentor. The coach oversees the mentor and student. This is where the individualized focus from the leg up program comes into play.
