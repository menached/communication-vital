Our Aim

To engage with, teach, and learn from a community of similarly language-challenged non-verbal adults and developmentally disabled individuals from all walks of life.

Our Challenge

Most parents face the enormous and emotionally draining prospect that there is no place for their aging family members to reside and thrive once they are no longer able to provide for them.

A Troubling Trend

Autism is a pervasive problem that is growing at an alarming rate. It is overwhelming for parents to try to continue teaching verbally challenged adults as they face unique difficulties, often leading to stress, anxiety, and potentially dangerous reactions.

Autism Affects Huge Numbers Today

Millions of adults in the US have Autism, with 25%-30% being minimally verbal. The numbers are growing, and many autistic individuals are reaching adulthood with limited options for continuing education.
